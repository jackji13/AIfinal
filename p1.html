How ChatGPT work

ChatGPT works like a super-smart assistant that understands and responds to your questions. When you type something, your words are turned into numbers that the system can process. These numbers are sent through layers of "thinking" steps called neural networks. Each layer looks for patterns and meaning in your input, like figuring out what you're asking and what makes sense to say next. ChatGPT is trained on a lot of information from books, websites, and conversations, so it can use that knowledge to give you a helpful answer.
