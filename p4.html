How does Runway work

Runway is like a virtual studio assistant built for creators. It specializes in helping you create videos, images, and other visual projects, offering tools that make the creative process fast and easy. For example, you can use Runway to edit videos, apply effects, or generate unique visuals with just a few clicks. It’s powered by models trained on video and image-specific data, so it knows how to handle tasks like resizing, enhancing, or adding creative elements. Whether you’re a solo artist or part of a team, Runway makes collaboration simple and brings your ideas to life efficiently.
